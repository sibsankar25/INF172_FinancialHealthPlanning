HSA Life Path

An educational game that helps you understand the basics of Health Savings Account (HSA)

What is HSA Life Path?

HSA Life Path is a life simulator game that allows users to better understand how to maximize their HSAs. The user is able to make decisions through different events, affecting their health, finances, and learn about HSA-eligible products. This short informational game aims to introduce the concept of HSA to our users.

Our Motivation

Health savings account (HSA) literacy among adults, especially college students, is low. As people graduate from college and start their careers, many become eligible for an HSA, and understanding how an HSA works becomes essential. Our goal is to better prepare college students for this step into financial independence, specifically through education about how HSAs work, how best to maximize them, and what exactly counts as HSA-eligible expenses.

Key Features

1

Simulated real-life events where HSA funds are applicable

2

Selectable options for game events affecting game stats

3

"Did you know?" messages to introduce fun facts about HSAs

4

Include numerous examples of HSA-eligible products
