Project Summary

An early-stage undergraduate research and design project exploring how interactive gameplay can improve HSA understanding among college students.

  • Audience: College Students
  • Approach: Gamified Interactive Scenarios
  • Status: Early-Stage Prototype
  • Course: IN4MATX 172 · Winter 2026

What is HSA Life Path?

A short informational game designed to introduce HSA concepts through play.

The Game

HSA Life Path is a life simulator where users experience how to maximize their HSAs firsthand. Make decisions through different life events — affecting your health, finances, and wellbeing — and learn about HSA-eligible products along the way.

Key Features

  • Simulated real-life events where HSA funds are applicable
  • Selectable options that affect in-game stats
  • "Did you know?" messages introducing HSA facts
  • Numerous examples of HSA-eligible products

Our Motivation

Why this project exists — and why it matters for students.

The Problem

HSA literacy among adults — especially college students — is low. As people graduate and start their careers, many become eligible for an HSA, but most have never been taught how one works.

Our Goal

Better prepare college students for financial independence — through education about how HSAs work, how to maximize them, and what counts as HSA-eligible expenses.

Research Snapshot

Preliminary survey of 26 undergraduate students.
