About the Project

The problem we set out to solve and how we approached it.

What we built

HSA Life Path is a scenario-based learning game designed to teach young adults the fundamentals of Health Savings Accounts. Players navigate simulated life events — from getting their first job to handling unexpected medical expenses — making decisions that affect health, stress, and savings in real time.

Why we built it

Our research showed that most undergraduates don't know what an HSA is, and almost none have one. Yet as they enter the workforce, these decisions matter. We wanted to create a low-stakes, engaging way to build that knowledge before it's needed.

IN4MATX 172 — User Interaction Software

This project was created as part of an undergraduate research and design course at UC Irvine, focused on applying user-centered design methodologies to real-world problems. The team completed three rounds of user research and three prototype iterations over the course of Winter 2026.
