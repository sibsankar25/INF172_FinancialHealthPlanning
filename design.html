Design Process

3 rounds of user research & 3 rounds of design iterations

Problem Statement

How might we design a simulation game that helps young adults understand the benefits of having an HSA account and the importance of financial planning?

User Story

"As a game player, I want to make choices based on HSA-related simulation life events, so that I can be more knowledgeable about how HSA works to better plan my finances."

Persona

We created a user persona to reflect on our user needs and requirements. Our character's name is Daniel. He is a current college student in his 2nd year of studies. He wants to learn more about HSA and related products. However, he finds current resources to be overwhelming and lack clear guidance on information related to HSA. We address his needs by creating an HSA educational game.

Limitations & Future Plans

Here are our limitations and some ideas for future implementations

Current Limitations

  • Personal Customization
    • Avatar Customization
    • Starting game conditions
      • Salary, savings, health, stress level, etc.
  • Limitations on HSA-related resources
    • If user wants to know more about HSA, they need to conduct their own research

Future Plans

  • Additional scenarios
    • 30+ scenarios + events
    • More information on HSA
    • Goal is to expand user's knowledge on HSA
  • Investment options
    • Option to invest in stocks, mutual funds, and ETFs
    • Real-life reflection of stock market
  • Expanded gameplay
    • 10 minutes short game → everyday game
